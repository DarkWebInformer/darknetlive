Policies

Byline Policy

Darknetlive News articles based on original reporting carry bylines (the name or chosen identity of the author), as often do those authored by authors who have a subject specialism. General news stories, which tend to combine information from a variety of sources, including HUMANINT or submitted content, do not as a rule carry bylines.

Removal or Ammendment of Content

Content should only be removed in exceptional circumstances. These may include legal reasons such as defamation or contempt, genuine safety risks to individuals, significant harm or distress to a contributor, or a serious breach of editorial standards.
