About Rapture Market
Rapture Market is a TradeRoute Market clone created by a darknet vendor namedBritish Dragonnow banned on Reddit. Along with his partner, British Dragon decided to launch a public marketplace instead of a vendor shop.The market offers the following features:A referral systemBitcoin and MoneroMulti-signature Payment optionsWarnings on FE ordersPublic feedback and discussion on buyers, vendors, and productsA top ten vendors panelBulk listings for vendorsA detailed vendor control panel
How to Become a Vendor on Rapture Market:
Before becoming a vendor you must read the following rules. Please remember that breaking any of this rules will lead to an automatic and permanent ban.Selling firearms, explosives or anything intended to harm others is prohibitedChild pornography is prohibitedOffering assasination services is prohibitedScamming is prohibited and will be strongly prosecuted*If you are a reputable vendor who sells on other marketplaces you can request a vendor bond waiver. Please configure your PGP key then send a message to support requesting the waiver and please dont forget to tell us in which marketplaces you sell.We currently charge a small 250 USD fee to become a full vendor, this is in order to stop scammers from registering as vendors. This quantity will be automatically refunded into your account once you close 100 successful sales or once you terminate your vendor activity.At the current rates the vendor bond costs 0.028351 BTC
