ONemesis is a vendor- and community-oriented darknet market that combines several successful features of its predecessors into a uniquely designed market/forum hybrid.10 days auto-finalize for physical orders and 3 day for digital orders, finalize early for established vendors, accepting Bitcoin and Monero, a nice forum, lower fees compare to other markets, free Bitcoin mixing service for vendors made this market a better option for a lot of vendors and buyers.Nemesis is a wallet-less market, so you do not need to deposit into your account and store extra money, or risk losing balance during crypto fluctuations. You can create an order with no balance, add multiple items to your cart and pay the exact amount.Nemesis has a very interesting referral program.
While 25% is the maximum offer from other markets, Nemesis Market offers a 50% referral program, and vendors are allowed to use it.
If a vendor shares his referral link, instead of paying a 5% commission fee will pay a 2.5% commission fee.Market doesnt have any withdrawal fees. There is only a 5% commission fee for vendors. No deposit fee, No withdrawal fee, No hidden fee, No buyers fee.A very interesting feature of Nemesis Market is they show reviews and sales of a vendor from other markets too, so buyers can see if a vendor is reputable or not
