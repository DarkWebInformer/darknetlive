Tripcodes
To use a tripcode, add a ## in your name. After the ##, add the password for the tripcode.The server will apply a hash to the password and display the hashed password.To use a secure tripcode, add #### in your name and a password. A secure tripcode will generate a random text to be displayed that will only be used if your password matches.
Role signature
If you wish to display your global or board role when posting, you can put ##rs in your name, like a tripcode, case insensitive. This will prevent you from using a tripcode.You can also enable an always use role signature rule in your account settings.
