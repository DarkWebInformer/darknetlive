A Former Tochka Mod Surfaced on Dread

~4 min read | Published on 2019-12-26, tagged Darkweb-Market, Moderator using 758 words.
A former moderator of the now-defunct Tochka/Point Marketplace asked other former staff members to get in touch. The mod wrote that he had only returned to ask about the market’s disappearance almost 30 days ago.
The former mod has not publicly posted or commented on Dread since April 2018, making the return somewhat unusual. The only official comment from Tochka staff came from /u/r00tedlucifer on December 11; the mod wrote that nobody had heard from the administrator and to “consider finding an alternative source for your future shopping.”
The message is below. A signed copy is available here in text format and here on Dread. The signature matches the PGP Key stored on only0ne’s profile.

Hello,

I am only0ne, a former mod of Tochka. I worked in 2017-2018 on Tochka, alongside /u/earthling and some other mods.

Due to personal problems (I got fucked in the Crypto-Crash, which mostly killed my private assets) I varnished in 2018 - I am still in complete control of my PGP Key.

This message mostly targets /u/earthling as I would like to know what happend to him. We had many interesting conversations while working together and I learned much while working with him. He is a very smart person from what I can tell.

I do not think that he exit-scammed, I also do not think that he went to jail. Maybe he is in the hospital?

I would like to know what happend, thats why I came back from the shadows and why I now write this message.

If you or any other former moderator who worked with me, reads this, feel free to message for a quick chat.

I will check the personal messages I receive on this account for at least the next 12 months.
