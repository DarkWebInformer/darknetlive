Alphabay Fentanyl Dealer UKBargins Confesses

~1 min read | Published on 2018-05-30, tagged Alphabay, Darkweb-Vendor, Pleaded-Guilty using 116 words.
Three men have admitted running the Alphabay vendor account where they sold fentanyl and dozens of fentanyl analogues. Investigators have traced their drugs back to six overdoses.
Since they sold and advertised the fentanyl as fentanyl, I personally do not think they are to blame for the fatal decisions their buyers made.
Jake Levene, 22, Lee Childs, 45, and Mandy Christopher Lowther, 21, mixed the drugs with bulking agents and then posted them to customers throughout the UK as well as the US, Germany, Norway, Canada, Australia, Argentina, Canada, France, Singapore, Holland and Spain.
UKBargins


  • Sold from December 2016 to April 2017;
  • Earned £163,474;
  • Sold to 443 customers;
  • Shipped almost 3,000 packages;
  • Contributed or caused six fatal overdoses.
