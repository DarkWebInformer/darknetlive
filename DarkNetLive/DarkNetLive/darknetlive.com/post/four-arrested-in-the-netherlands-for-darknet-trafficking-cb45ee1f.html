Four Arrested in the Netherlands for Darknet Trafficking

~1 min read | Published on 2018-05-19, tagged Arrested, Darkweb-Vendor, Drug-Bust, Drugs using 133 words.
Under the direction of the National Public Prosecution Service of the Netherlands, the National Police arrested four darknet drug dealers on Tuesday in Limburg for trafficking ecstasy via the darkweb. From Limburg, the four men and allegedly produced and shipped ecstasy tablets. The suspects will be brought before a judge on Friday.
These four will soon land on the arrested darknet vendor list.
The investigation into the origin of the packages started after PostNL discovered packages of drugs during a drug investigation in 2017. The investigation led to the discovery of men from Brunssum of the following ages:

  • a 24-year-old;
  • a 27-year-old;
  • a 35-year-old;
  • and a 53-year-old.
