New Vendor Update: U.S. Police Launch their Own Tor Site for Darkweb Vendor Busts

~1 min read | Published on 2019-06-11, tagged General-News using 219 words.
Not unlike the hidden services posted by Dutch law enforcement with lists of identified vendors, law enforcement in the United States recently published their own hidden service with a list of vendors arrested by law enforcement in California. The problem: some people are still buying from the vendors listed on the hidden service.
Update: the Task-Force added three more vendors to the list, including one prolific CGMC vendor. The new ones are listed under the “Identified NorCal Vendors” heading.
Although the majority of the vendors listed on the hidden service have been added to this site’s list of arrested vendors, a handful of them slipped through the cracks. According to a source who keeps a close eye on the community, some buyers still have active accounts on darkweb markets and customers are still attempting to purchase from these vendors.
