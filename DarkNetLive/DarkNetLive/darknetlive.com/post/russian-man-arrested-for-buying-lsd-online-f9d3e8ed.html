Russian Man Arrested for Buying LSD Online

~1 min read | Published on 2018-06-02, tagged Arrested, DarkWeb, Drug-Bust, Drugs using 119 words.
Anti-drug trafficking agents of Russia’s Ministry of Internal Affairs arrested a 35-year-old from Elista for ordering LSD from a vendor on the internet.
The press release explained that the man had used the internet to order a small amount of LSD. The man provided a vendor with his address. Police intercepted the package at as it entered the city of Elista.

Police then searched the suspect’s house and seized computers, cellphones, and other devices with internet access. One of the seized devices contained evidence pertinent the investigation. The announcement mentioned a “popular messaging platform” or application.
