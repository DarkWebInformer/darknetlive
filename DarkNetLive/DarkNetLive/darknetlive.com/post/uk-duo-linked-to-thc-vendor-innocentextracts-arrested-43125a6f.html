UK Duo Linked to THC Vendor "innocentextracts" Arrested

~1 min read | Published on 2023-12-25, tagged Arrested, Darkweb-Vendor using 167 words.
The West Midlands Police arrested a man and a woman as part of an ongoing investigation into the sale of illicit drugs through the dark web.

Some of innocentextracts' listings


The 27-year-old man and 26-year-old woman from Birmingham were taken in for questioning over suspicion of being involved in the distribution of class B drugs via the "innocentextracts" vendor profile on dark web marketplaces.
The West Midlands Police said in a press release that the duo's arrest resulted in the seizure of "numerous" drug packages for forensic evaluation.
The police disclosed that the arrest and questioning of the duo resulted in the launch of an investigation against innocentextracts.
Innocentextracts' profile on Incognito says the vendor operates from the UK. The vendor distributes THC products.
Investigations into the vendor are part of an operation named Operation Illuminator. The Regional Organised Crime Unit set up the operation and aims to uncover the identities of individuals who engage in illicit activities through the dark web.
The man and woman were released on bail after being questioned.
