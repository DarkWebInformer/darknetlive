Unsuccessful Porn Actress Busted for Selling Counterfeit Oxys

~2 min read | Published on 2022-05-14, tagged Arrested, Counterfeits, Darkweb-Vendor, Drug-Bust, Drugs using 280 words.
In the Eastern District of California, a grand jury indicted two people for allegedly selling counterfeit oxycodone pills through the darkweb.

Holly Adams dropping off packages of fake oxys at a post office in California.



According to court records, 31-year-old Holly Adams and 33-year-old Devlin Hosner were charged with conspiracy to distribute and possess with intent to distribute fentanyl and methamphetamine for selling drugs through vendor accounts on the ToRReZ and Dark0de marketplaces. They allegedly operated vendor accounts with the usernames “igogrraawwr” and “its4real.” Both suspects also face money laundering charges.
During the investigation into the suspected vendors, investigators with the Northern California Illicit Digital Economy (NCIDE) Task Force discovered an account on a pornography website with the username “igogrraawwr.” The account belonged to Adams.

...



Investigators conducted five undercover purchases of oxycodone from “igogrraawwr” on ToRReZ market between May 2021 and January 2022. Surveillance cameras captured Adams mailing packages of drugs, including one of the packages addressed to undercover law enforcement officers.

The so-called “sex-worker” drove a prius, which police spotted on surveillance footage.



Coinbase records indicated that Hosner had cashed out $800,000 worth of Bitcoin in 2021.
On March 29, investigators executed a search warrant at a hotel where Adams and Hosner resided. The search resulted in the seizure of more than 10,000 counterfeit oxycodone pills and approximately 60 grams of methamphetamine.
